THE SPIRIT OF COUNSEL

The word Estah essentially means

  • " to counsel"
  • "to advise"
  • "to direct"

Estah’s essence lies in the propagation of wisdom and sustainability through the spirit of counselling.

ESTAH operates in various villages in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, and TamilNadu and works towards the training and counselling of farmers in the execution of fundamental practices like natural farming, horticulture, and economically sustainable farming methods, which is the primary impelling cause of our organization
